About GHATI ADDA

This place is famous for it's Thalis,located in belapur this place serves one of the best thalis in the town.The ambience is very soothing & calm with soft music,the interior is not very fancy but is very cozy & seating is quite comfortable.Coming to the food,we had ordered Chicken tandoori as starter, chicken was tender with correct amount of marination,we then ordered chicken thali,they serves Bhakri(you can also order chapati),dry chicken, chicken curry,egg curry,pandhra & tambda rassa,sol kadi,brown rice(after bhakri) & salad.

Bhakris were soft & were huge in size,dry chicken & curry both were tasty but my favorite was pandhra rassa it had perfect consistency & taste,the rice had a really nice fregrence,tasted good with the curry.If you're looking for a place to have dinner with soft music & good non veg food this place should be on your list,though I haven't tried their other dishes I didn't see any kind of negetive points in this restaurant, overall 4/5
